Our Transparent Process

Leading the industry in secure and reliable property management.

1. Application & Fee

The $150 application fee initiates a national background check, credit analysis, and income verification. This is a non-refundable administrative cost for professional screening.

2. Digital Verification

We use 256-bit encryption to verify your employment and rental history. Most applications are processed within 48 business hours.

3. Residency Agreement

Approved residents receive a digital lease. Upon signing and security deposit payment, keys are delivered via our secure remote-access system.

Terms & Residency Policies

  • Non-Refundable Fee: All applicants acknowledge that the $150.00 fee is for background processing and is non-refundable regardless of approval status.
  • Income Standard: Total household gross income must be at least 3x the monthly rent.
  • Occupancy: Only individuals listed on the approved application may reside in the property.
  • Maintenance: Residents are responsible for reporting all maintenance needs via the digital portal within 24 hours of discovery.
