How We Work: Our 4-Step Process

1. Property Curation & Inspection

Every home in our national network undergoes a rigorous "Gold Standard" inspection. We only manage properties that meet strict safety, aesthetic, and functional requirements. If we wouldn't live there ourselves, we won't rent it to you.

2. Professional Screening & Verification

The $150 application fee covers a comprehensive national background check, credit history analysis, and income verification. This ensures that every Hogar Dulce community is comprised of reliable, verified residents.

3. Digital Leasing & Onboarding

Once approved, your entire lease is managed through our secure cloud portal. No paper, no stress. You can review, sign, and store your documents digitally, and your keys are released via our secure remote-access lockbox system.

4. Lifetime Resident Support

Managing a property doesn't end when you move in. Our 24/7 maintenance dispatch and dedicated state-level property managers are always a click away to ensure your home remains in peak condition.

Terms of Residency & Privacy Policy

By applying for a Hogar Dulce Rentals property, all applicants agree to the following institutional terms:


  • Application Fee: The $150.00 application fee is non-refundable and covers the cost of credit reporting, criminal background checks, and administrative processing.
  • Income Requirements: Applicants must demonstrate a gross monthly income of at least 3x the monthly rent of the desired property.
  • Privacy & Security: All sensitive data (SSNs, Bank Info, IDs) is encrypted using 256-bit SSL technology. We never sell your data to third parties.
  • Occupancy Limits: Properties are restricted to the occupants listed on the application. Unauthorized long-term guests are a violation of the lease terms.
  • Pet Policy: Individual property pet rules vary. All pets must be disclosed, and additional pet rent or deposits may apply based on breed and weight.
  • Maintenance Responsibility: While we cover major structural and system repairs, residents are responsible for basic upkeep and reporting issues immediately to prevent property damage.
